The city of light, the capital of fashion and beauty, and also the city of love. These are all titles given to Paris, one of the most important cultural and economic capitals in the world, whose ancient history and unique arts have influenced the entire world.

Popular destinations in paris

Paris, the capital of beauty and the destination of those with good taste, the city of light, the capital of fashion and beauty, and also the city of love.

This massive building is a favorite building for many visitors in the city, but there are also many beautiful museums to explore, not to mention the Gros Horloge clock tower, France's oldest inn La Couronne and the beautiful modern church dedicated to Joan of Arc.

The French city of Nantes is located in the western part of France. It overlooks the Loire River from its western side. It also occupies an area extending from the beginning of the river’s mouth to the end of its mouth in the Atlantic Ocean. The city’s lands are interspersed with two tributaries of the river: the Ardre and the Nantes Sèvres. The city of Nantes is still 342 km² to the southwest from the French capital, Paris, and the city of Bordeaux is 340 km to the north. The city of Nantes is considered the administrative director of the Loire-Atlantique region.
